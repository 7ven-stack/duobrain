How to Play

The Basics

DuoBrain is a fast-paced 1v1 trivia battle. The Host selects the category and difficulty. You have 20 seconds to lock in your answer. Answer correctly to earn a point!

Wager Phase Round

Before Round 5 begins, you will see the category and must bet 1, 2, or 3 points. Answer correctly to win those points, but guess wrong and you lose them! This is your chance for a massive comeback.

Sudden Death Round

The final round is Sudden Death! The pressure is doubled with only 7 seconds on the clock. If both players answer correctly, the player who answered fastest steals the point.

Power-Ups

You can use each power-up exactly once per match. Use them strategically to disrupt your opponent or save yourself!

  • Decrypt: Instantly removes two incorrect answers from your screen.
  • Overclock: Adds 8 seconds to your timer, buying you more time to think.
  • Glitch: Blurs your opponent's screen and shakes their answers, causing chaos!
