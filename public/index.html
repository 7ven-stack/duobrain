How to Play

🎯 The Basics

DuoBrain is a fast-paced 1v1 trivia battle. The Host selects the category and difficulty. You have 20 seconds to lock in your answer. The first to answer correctly wins the round's point!

⚡ Tie-Breakers & Sudden Death

If both players answer correctly, the player who answered fastest gets the point. Round 6 is always Sudden Death—the pressure is doubled with only 7 seconds on the clock!

🔮 Power-Ups

You can use each power-up exactly once per match. Use them strategically to disrupt your opponent or save yourself!

  • 🔓 Decrypt: Instantly removes two incorrect answers from your screen.
  • ⚡ Overclock: Adds 8 seconds to your timer, buying you more time to think.
  • 👾 Glitch: Blurs your opponent's screen and shakes their answers, causing chaos!
